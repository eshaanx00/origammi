Origami instructions and diagrams


Origami is the art of paper folding, which is often associated with Japanese culture. In modern usage, the word "origami" is used as an inclusive term for all folding practices, regardless of their culture of origin

interesting facts about camel

  1. There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  2. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  3. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can’t eat.

interesting facts about flying cicada

  1. Most have red-orange eyes. But occasionally cicadas have blue, white or grayish eyes. Keep on the lookout.
  2. They’re true bugs (from the order Hemiptera). So you can call them a bug and be scientifically accurate.
  3. Females may be attracted to the sound of motors. So you get to watch your dad, mom or older sibling get swarmed while they’re using the lawn mower or power tools.
